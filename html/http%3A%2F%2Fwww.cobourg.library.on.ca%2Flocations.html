Branch Locations

The C. Gordon King Centre was opened in 1995, and the 17,750 square feet facility includes a two-storey library, the Cobourg & District Historical Society Archives, and public meeting rooms to accommodate community and small business groups.

Located within minutes of Highway 401, in the heart of beautiful Cobourg, Ontario, close to the Heritage Harbour, parks, the waterfront trail, shopping and great restaurants.

Library services are also available at two Hamilton Township locations in Bewdley and Gore's Landing.
