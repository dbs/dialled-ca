    Get Involved, Cobourg

    We've been working diligently on our Public Engagement and Community Consultation Plan and Policy. It is our goal that citizens of all ages are meaningfully engaged in civic life where their voices strengthen decisions and their involvement helps build community where they live, work, learn, and play. 

    Connect with Cobourg. Learn more...

    You spoke, we acted...

    Many of our services are now offered online such as; paying for recreation programs, dog licenses, reporting a pothole, and much more.

    More than 2,349 people follow us on Facebook and have provided valuable input on several services, programs and initiatives we've launched.

    The public helped us with the re-design, functionality and navigation of our new website. Thank you!

    You spoke and we listened! We delivered a comprehensive Budget Plan in order to enhance the budget process.
