v) Duplicating

The Centre will copy any non copyrighted audio or video cassettes. The only charge is for the cost of the tape.

vi) Laminating

  • The machine is 25 inches wide and costs 20 cents per running foot.
  • If you do your own laminating, you pay only for the laminate you use.
  • If Media Centre staff does the laminating, there is a cost of $5.00 per 1/2 hour labour charge
