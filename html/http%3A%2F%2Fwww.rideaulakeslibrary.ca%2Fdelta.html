Delta Branch

 18 King Street • Delta, ON K0E 1G0 • 613-928-2991 • delta@rideaulakeslibrary.ca

Branch Highlights

In 2011, this small branch received a complete makeover with new shelving, seating and computer stations.  Delta in the future will be the location of a mini-Archive for the area featuring local histories, genealogies and archival materials and a microfilm reader.


Description & History

This branch opened in 1995 to replace an older branch that stood on the main street of the village.
