Resource Library

The Laurie Briggs Library is home to many resources related to learning disabilities including books, books on CD, DVD's and learning tools.  You can browse and borrow materials on a variety of topics including; Career & Education Transitions, Literacy, Math Strategies, ADHD, Dyslexia, Social Skills, Anxiety, Parenting Children with LDs.

Searching for a particular book?
To see if we have a specific title, you can search our online database by following these steps:

1. Go to: www.librarything.com/profile/LDAYLibrary

2. Click on "Your Library"

3. In the “Search this library” tab, type in a particular title that you would like to search.
