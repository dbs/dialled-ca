Healthy Workplaces. Healthy Workers. Healthy Communities.

The MFL Occupational Health Centre (OHC) is a community health centre funded by Winnipeg Regional Health Authority and donations. The Centre helps workers, employers, and joint health and safety committees to improve workplace health and safety conditions and eliminate hazards.

Date:

Occupational Health Centre is hosting a Free Public Presentation in March:

Poster

April 27th Full Day Workshop - Psychological Health and Safety in the Workplace: Exploring the New National Standard Together

Date:
Apr 27, 2016 | 9:15 am - 4:00 pm

Location:
Room 103 – Union Centre | 275 Broadway

Occupational Health Centre offers this full day workshop:

Space is limited so register early! 

Maximum two individuals per organization

Free of Charge — Registration is required

Registration Form

This training session will help you:

  • Understand the important differences between mental health, mental illness and  overall well-being as it relates to assessing and dealing with psychological hazards in your workplace
  • Learn communication tools to assist workers, supervisors and managers when offering support to co-workers who might be dealing with a mental health problem and related stigma
  • Recognize key factors and elements needed to begin constructing an organizational system dedicated to the protection of psychological health and safety while on-the-job
  • Acquire resources, ideas and practical tools that you can take back to your health and safety committee and other workplace champions to promote positive change
