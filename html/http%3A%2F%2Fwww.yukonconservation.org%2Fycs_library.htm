YCS Library

 

We produce quality publications including environmental education handbooks, trail guides, nature appreciation booklets, and our quarterly newsletter.

We maintain a library of books, films and other resource materials relating to energy conservation, northern development, environmental planning and management, and many other topics. The library is open for public use.

We also have publications and videos for sale and loan. Drop by YCS to view our selection or to browse the library.
