Labour Programs - Labour Board

The Library

The Board's library houses a small collection of texts, journals, reports and other publications dealing with industrial relations and labour law in Manitoba and other Canadian jurisdictions. The library's collection also includes the full text of the Board's Written Reasons for Decision issued since the 1970's. Since 1985, all arbitration awards, reported and unreported, and collective agreements in the province are filed with the Manitoba Labour Board.

Items from the collection are for reference use only and can be viewed in the Board's office. Copies of library material can be made in accordance with the fee schedule. Requests for material are also accepted in writing by fax at 204-945-1296 or by e-mail to mlb@gov.mb.ca.

Hours of Service

The public is welcome to visit the library. The hours are Monday to Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
