Open Learning

Open Learning provides distance learning for everyone. Our online and distance courses and programs may be completed anytime, anywhere. Gain a new career and a new perspective while maintaining a flexible schedule.

About Online and Distance Education at TRU

The online and distance education provider of Thompson Rivers University allows you to access accredited post-secondary courses wherever you live and work. Enrolment is always open, and our flexible scheduling options let you complete your courses on your timeline. If you’re returning to school, we specialize in assessing prior education and work experience so you don’t have to start from scratch.

Featured Online and Distance Education Programs

Bachelor of General Studies

This degree provides a flexible structure, which allows students to create their own program. TRU-OL offers a wide range of courses that fulfill degree requirements.

Learn more

Bachelor of Technology

This degree program provides technologists with the knowledge and skills necessary to become effective project leaders, supervisors, and managers in organizations within a changing business and technical environment.

Learn more

Bachelor of Health Science

The Bachelor of Health Science program provides health care diploma students and graduates from recognized programs and institutions with the opportunity to obtain a bachelor's degree.

Learn more

 

Why TRU Open Learning?

Online education is not always open education. Open Learning specializes in open. This means everyone may apply regardless of their previous experience, and anyone may enrol in courses for their own interest, professional development or to transfer the credit to their program at another school.

Everyone's path is different. Whichever way you go, we're with you at every step. #FindYourTRU.
