October 16, 2015 to March 26, 2016.
The Western Canada Theatre enters its fifth decade of producing national-calibre live theatre here in Kamloops and to celebrate the KMA invites residents to the opening reception of Front and Centre; Western Canada Theatre at 40 on Friday, October 16th, 2015 from 5:30 to 7 pm.

Front and Centre; Western Canada Theatre at 40 is a showcase of costumes, props, videos, interactives and interviews, along with an evolving selection of digitized materials from the fascinating collection of WCTâ€™s records including screenplays, correspondences and promotions.

Front and Centre derives from a place of relative honour within a theatre: the first row of auditorium seating at centre stage - the best seats in the house. Founded in 1975, the Company has rightfully claimed a place at the front and centre of cultural life both within and beyond our region. This exhibition puts their storied past in the spotlight.
