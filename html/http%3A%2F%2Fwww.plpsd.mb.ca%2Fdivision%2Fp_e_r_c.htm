PORTAGE EDUCATIONAL RESOURCE CENTRE (P.E.R.C.) GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

That PERC as a local physical facility was created as a means of furthering teacher professional development and, hence, improving the quality of education offered in the Division, and that the goals and objectives be as follows:

1. That PERC be a resource for a beginning teacher's continuing education, including initiation into the school division, and ongoing professional development.

2. That PERC be a vehicle for the continuing education of teachers, including the provision of a variety of activities designed to facilitate the professional growth of teachers.

3. That PERC encourage the Faculties of Education to utilize PERC for diversification of teacher education as part of field-based programs.

4. That PERC be a vehicle for educational research, including projects related to the improvement of classroom instruction as well as education innovation and reform.

5. That PERC be used as follows:

i) in the acquisition and co-ordination of specialized teaching aids not available to all schools;

ii) as a support service to teachers in the preparation of teaching aids and other materials intended to improve the quality of education;

iii) as support for the on-going development of school library services;

iv) as on-going clerical support for the local teachers' organization.

Adopted: Jan. 14/82
