FAQ's

Q: Can Heritage request transcripts on my behalf?

A: No, Heritage cannot contact a school and request transcripts. You must contact the school(s) to request a transcript.

Q: What transcripts do I send to Heritage?

A: You must send transcripts from any school that you have attended.
