Phelps Library
Redbridge Ontario

Phelps public library is open three times a week. Our library staff is made up of community volunteers. Two computers complement the book service available through the library. There is wireless internet service that is available at the library. Bring your lap top and use it inside or outside the building. We have interlibrary loans available for the books that are not here. Come out and visit us.
Join and use your library and its services.

Hours of Operation:

Tuesday and Thursday - 7:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.
Saturday 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
