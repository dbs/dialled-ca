SPECIAL  ANNOUNCEMENT

The Harry Sutherland Library at the Vincent Graves Greene Philatelic Research Foundation is pleased to announce the dates for our Spring EXTENDED HOURS and OPENING DAYS. In addition to our regular opening hours, Monday through Thursday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m., you can now research at the V.G.G. on the fourth Wednesday of the month prior to the G.T.A.P.A. meetings from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m. and on the following Saturdays from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.

April 9th 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.   April 27th 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.

May 7th 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.   May 25th 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.

June 11th 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.  June 22nd 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.

Saturday GTAPA Wednesday.

Email library@greenefoundation.ca to be placed on our Friends of the HSPL mailing list, or telephone us at (416) 921-2073 for more information.
