Need a Boost?
Do you need a charge but forgot your cable?
  Need a fast boost and have your cable?
Our "Powerqube" charging station can quick-charge any smart phone or tablet.
We provide cables for iPhone/iPod/iPad 8-pin and 30-pin devices, Blackberry, Samsung Galaxy Tablets, and most Android devices.
Inquire at the main desk for the charging station and/or required cable.
Please note:  for use in the Library only.
  This equipment is offered to you by the Ontario Libraries Capacity Fund from the Ministry of Tourism, Culture and Sport.

Our Mission
The mission of the Meaford Public Library is to open doors to a world of information, education and recreation, thereby enhancing the economic, social and cultural vitality of our community.

Our Vision
The Meaford Public Library is the hub for knowledge and forward thinking, where everyone feels welcome and community pride.
