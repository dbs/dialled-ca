The Richard & Beryl Ivey Visual Resources Library (VRL)

Located on the second floor of the John Labatt Visual Arts Centre, The Richard & Beryl Ivey Visual Resources Library (VRL) supports teaching and research in the Department of Visual Arts by providing instructors and students with access to high-quality teaching resources.

Included in the VRL collections are films, periodicals, 35mm slides, and over 50,000 digital images. For information onrecent acquisitions and visual resources news, visit the VRL blog.
