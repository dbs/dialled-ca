About Us

Hours | Loan Policy | Contact Us | Map & Directions

Hours:

Hours for the Fire Sciences Library & Audio-Visual Resource Centre
Monday to Friday: 8:30am - 4:15pm

The library is open to the fire service, public sector and broader public sector entities by appointment only. Please call library staff at 647-329-1112.

Loan Policy:

Fire Sciences Library & Audio-Visual Resource Centre
Materials are loaned to OFMEM staff, the fire service, public sector and broader public sector entities. Other clients must arrange for an interlibrary loan through their municipal public library or institutional library.

Resource Centre, Ontario Fire College
Materials are for reference use at the Fire College by students and instructors. Library clients outside of the OFM must request interlibrary loan service through public libraries or institutional libraries.

Map & Directions:

Please click here for a map to the Fire Sciences Library & Audio-Visual Resource Centre located at 25 Morton Shulman Avenue in the Office of the Fire Marshal and Emergency Management on the 2nd Floor in Toronto, Ontario M3M 1J8.

From East or West: Travel on Highway 401 West and take the Keele Street exit Northbound. The Office of the Fire Marshal is located just south of Wilson Avenue on the west side. Parking is available at street level. Please obtain a visitor's badge in the main lobby.

Please click here for a map to the Resource Centre located at the Ontario Fire College, 1495 Muskoka Road North in Gravenhurst, Ontario P1P 1R8.

From the South: Travel North on Highway 11 to Gravenhurst. Take exit 169 on the left for County Road 169 toward Bethune Drive in Gravenhurst. Turn left at Muskoka Road South, and continue through town to Muskoka Road North. The Ontario Fire College is on the left.
