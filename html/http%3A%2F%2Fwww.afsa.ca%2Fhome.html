Welcome

The Alberta Funeral Service Association, a professional organization of funeral service providers in the Province of Alberta, is dedicated to the betterment of funeral service through support to the public, as well as to our members. 

The Alberta Funeral Service Assocation's commitment to public support is provided through brochures, public seminars, a web-site and telephone information lines. Membership support is provided by relevant and current information on all issues, concerans and details impacting fiuneral service, through resource books and a newsletter. Continuing education seminars and gatherings facilitate learning experiences, the exchange of ideas, camaraderie and mutual respect amongst the membership.

Member Firms 
Our membership continues to grow throughout the province and our member funeral providers are listed by area. If you would like more information about our members, please click here.

The Strength of Membership 
We are committed to providing high quality services to our members and representing their interests through constructive dialogue and consultation with all levels of Government. Learn More.

Helping You Plan

At your difficult time, it's important to know that the firm you are choosing represents the highest of standards and professionalism, by the very nature of their association with the Alberta Funeral Service Association.

Helping You Grieve

The strength in membership comes in the form of strength in numbers. ASFA represents a coalition of professional funeral homes who believe that a solid future for funeral service is based on the collective insight and dedication from caring professionals. This unified voice helps to shape and provide the professional standards that families and communities count on in their time of need.
