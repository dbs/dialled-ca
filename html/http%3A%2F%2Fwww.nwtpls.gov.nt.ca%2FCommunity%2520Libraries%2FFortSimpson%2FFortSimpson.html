John Tsetso Memorial Library Welcomes You!

NWT Community Libraries

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

The John Tsetso Memorial Library located in Fort Simpson provides access to library services to meet your educational, cultural, recreational and informational needs. Please call (695-3276) for information on hours and programs.

What do we do?

We provide a comfortable reading and learning environment. A great place for families to read and learn together.

We have books, audiobooks, DVDs, magazines, and online databases to meet your educational needs as well as your recreational desires.

We provide free computer and internet use.

We provide library and literacy programs.
