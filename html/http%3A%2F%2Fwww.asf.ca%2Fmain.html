Fascinating Science
For ASF, Science is more than 9 to 5 in a lab. It is being on the water through rain, wind, and sometimes travelling on flooded rivers. Patience is required when waiting out high winds in order to deploy and pick up tracking devices at sea.

The Mystery of Atlantic Salmon

For centuries no one knew where wild Atlantic salmon travelled at sea. Did they follow food, or migrate to specific areas of the ocean? 2012 may be an important year, as for the first time both exits of the Gulf of St. Lawrence are wired to identify Atlantic salmon with ASF's acoustic tags.

Where to See Atlantic Salmon

From Maine to Newfoundland, there are more than a dozen places offering opportunities to see Atlantic salmon and to learn about the cultural heritage surrounding them.


This list includes many underwater viewing opportunities, as well as ways to learn more about Atlantic salmon restoration work, and the long history of angling in many of these rivers.
