 WHAT WE DO

provide ALL MEMBERS Free Access to:

  • over 39,000 books, DVDs and other items
  • an online catalogue at www.rideaulakeslibrary.ca where patrons can search the library catalogue, place holds and renew items at any time
  • over 1,000,000 eBooks through Overdrive
  • a unique Local History Collection
  • one-on-one technology help, wireless internet and computer access at all branches
  • quality reference resources such as: Ancestry Library Edition; Novelist - a book lover's resource; and Chilton Automotive Repairs
  • online courses through our partnership with Contact North, Ontario's Distance Education & Training Network
  • public library material throughout Ontario through the interlibrary loan service
  • family passes to many Ottawa museums

                    

offer the following services to ADULTS and SENIORS:

  • support to 14 book clubs
  • weekly technology workshops
  • monthly card making workshop and weekly Hooks & Books fibre club
  • outreach service to homebound residents

 

deliver the following programs to FAMILIES, CHILDREN and TEENS:

  • weekly story time for young children and their care-givers
  • holiday programs such as March Break, Christmas and Valentine's Day

               

  • visits to local schools, Rideau Lakes Day Camp, and Elgin Cooperative Nursery School
  • the TD Summer Reading Club, summer Reading Rescue help, and tutoring space
  • weekly Tweens Book Club and Make-it and Teen Writers Club
  • the popular Read to Every Kid Every Day

The Friends of the Rideau Lakes Public Library welcome new volunteers willing to share their talents and skills.  For further information on becoming a Friend, sign up at your local library branch or contact us at friends.rideaulakeslibrary@gmail.com.

To keep up to date on library programs, please follow us on Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/rideaulakespubliclibrary.
