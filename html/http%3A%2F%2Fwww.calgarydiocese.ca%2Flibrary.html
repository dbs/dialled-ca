Calgary Catholic Pastoral Centre Library

About the Library

Grow in faith! The Library has a wealth of resources available to you. We collect books, audio CDs and DVDs to lend on the following:
  • Bible study
  • Christian ethics and social justice
  • Spirituality and prayer
  • How to work and volunteer in your parish
  • Liturgy and sacraments
  • Forming faithful families and communities
  • How to become a saints
  • Passing on our faith

Library Services

Membership

To join the library you will need to fill out an application form and show a piece of photo ID. There is no cost to join if you are a parishioner within the Diocese. If an item is lost or damaged while in your care we reserve the right to charge you for the cost of replacement.

Loans

Most of our resources are available for loan.

  • Books are normally checked out for 4 weeks
  • Videos and DVDs for 1 week

These loan periods are negotiable if you have special circumstances. Just call us!
