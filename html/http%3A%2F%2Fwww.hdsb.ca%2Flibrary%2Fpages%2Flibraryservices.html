About Library Services
Read about our services below. Questions - contact Manager of Instructional Media, Lynn Wisniewski or Manager of Library Technical Services, Yunmi Hwang.

Department Goals
  • To promote reading and life-long learning
  • To provide technical and administrative support and training to school library staff
  • To support and engage students and staff in meeting the expectations and objectives of the Ontario curriculum
  • To maintain a current and broad collection of library resources for students and staff
  • To foster leadership in librarianship and multiple literacies
  • To ensure the ethical use of information resources
  • To embrace, adapt to, and adopt 21st century technologies in school libraries
  • To create an awareness of, and where possible, access to, information resources beyond school library collections
  • To advocate for and promote library services
  • To create and maintain library facilities that are organized and engaging with a focus to encourage collaboration and learning
Cataloguing & Processing
  • Provides operational support to the Horizon integrated library system
  • Provides full training sessions for HDSB staff in the use of the Horizon Information Portal, the Horizon circulation system and the Horizon Web Reporter system
  • Provides full cataloguing and processing services for all schools, including bulk orders
  • Provides technical support and consultation services for library projects such as weeding and re-locating collections
  • Provides barcodes, library cards and barcode readers to school libraries
For additional information, please contact the Manager of Technical Services.

Education Library
  • Includes resources for areas such as professional development, educational theory, literacy, and librarianship
  • Includes videos for both teacher professional development and classroom instruction
  • Includes Ministry of Education and Training curriculum and resource documents in electronic format
  • Provides an adult fiction collection in audio and ebook formats
  • Provides access to OSAPAC licensed computer (Mac and PC) software
  • Access to the collection is available though the Education Library Catalogue for HDSB staff
Please contact the Education Library Technician for further information.

Interlibrary Loan
If the information you require is not available at the Education Library, you may want to request an interlibrary loan. Please fill out our Interlibrary Loan Request form and return it to Library Services. For additional information, please contact the Education Library Technician.

Research Assistance
Research assistance is available for HDSB staff. Please contact the Online/Web Library Technician for an appointment or fill out our Research Request form and return it to Library Services. For additional information, please contact the the Online/Web Library Technician.

Training
Training is available upon request to HDSB staff for a number of library-related topics. Training can be for either a one-on-one or group session. Sessions may be held in schools or at the J.W. Singleton Education Centre. Support materials will be provided for all training sessions.
  • For HDSB-supported information databases, for general or project-specific uses, contact our Online/Web Library Technician
  • For copyright information, contact our Manager of Instructional Media
  • For the Horizon library automation system, contact our Library Information Systems Technician
  • For school library inventory procedures, contact our Library Information Systems Technician
  • For video streaming, contact our Education Library Technician
